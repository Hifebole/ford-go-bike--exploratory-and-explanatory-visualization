This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area. It contains the duration of trips, the start and end time of trip, information about the location of start and end station of trips, user info like type of user (subscriber or customer), gender and age

Conclusions¶

With the aid of visualisation, I was a able to get some insights from the dataset, some of which are

  • Seniors adults from ages 60 above have higher ride durations than middle age adults and young adults, this could be attributed to the fact that , this most users in this age group would mostly be retired and therefore would have more time to spend on cycling than the younger adults

  • The young adults are the lowest in terms if subscribers , this is probably due to their unpredictible schedules compaired to the older adults and the tendecy to have less committment to cycling

Limitations¶

The dataset covers only the month of february , I believe it would have being more insightful to use data that covers a whole year
